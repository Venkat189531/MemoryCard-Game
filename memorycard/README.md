## ğŸ® How to Play

1. **Open the Game**: Launch `index.html` in your web browser.
2. **Start Matching**: Click on any card to flip it over and reveal the hidden icon.
3. **Find the Pair**: Click another card to find its match.
   - **Match**: Cards stay face up.
   - **Mismatch**: Cards flip back down after a short delay.
4. **Win**: Clear the entire board by matching all pairs.
5. **Beat Your Score**: Try to win with fewer moves and in less time!
